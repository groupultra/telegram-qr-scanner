What are Telegram QR Codes?

Telegram QR codes are a quick way to share links to Telegram users, groups, or channels. By scanning these QR codes, you can instantly add friends, join groups, or subscribe to channels without having to manually search or type usernames.

The official Telegram app and most third-party clients (like Nicegram) support generating and scanning QR codes. This tutorial will explain in detail how to use Telegram's QR scanning feature on different devices.

Scanning Telegram QR Codes on Mobile

1

Open Telegram App

Open the Telegram app on your phone and make sure you're logged in.

2

Find the QR Scanner

There are two ways to find Telegram's QR scanning feature:

Method 1: Via Search Bar
  1. Tap the search icon (magnifying glass) in the top right corner
  2. Look for the QR code icon on the right side of the search bar
  3. Tap the QR code icon to open the scanning feature
Method 2: Via Contacts Page
  1. Tap Contacts in the bottom navigation bar
  2. Find the QR code icon in the top right corner of the contacts page
  3. Tap the QR code icon to open the scanning feature
3

Scan the QR Code

Point your phone's camera at the Telegram QR code, keep it steady, and wait for automatic recognition. Make sure there's adequate lighting and the QR code is clearly visible.

4

View Scan Results

After successful scanning, Telegram will display the corresponding user, group, or channel information. Depending on the QR code type, you can:

  • Tap Add Contact to add a user as a friend
  • Tap Join to join a group
  • Tap View or Join Channel to view or subscribe to a channel

How to Generate Your Own Telegram QR Code

1

Open Telegram App

Open the Telegram app on your phone and make sure you're logged in.

2

Go to Settings

Tap the Settings icon (⚙️) in the bottom left corner.

3

View Your Profile

Tap your Profile at the top of the settings page.

4

Generate QR Code

On your profile page, tap the three-dot menu in the top right corner, then select the QR Code option. Telegram will generate your personal QR code, which other users can scan to add you as a friend.

Frequently Asked Questions About Telegram QR Scanning

Where is the QR scanner in Telegram?

In the Telegram app, tap the search icon in the top right corner, then you'll see a QR code icon on the right side of the search bar. Tap it to open the QR scanner. In some versions, you can also find the QR code icon in the top right corner of the Contacts page.

Why can't I scan Telegram QR codes?

Possible reasons include:

  • Camera permissions not enabled
  • Unclear or damaged QR code image
  • Insufficient lighting
  • Network connection issues
  • Outdated Telegram app version

We recommend checking these issues, or try using our website to scan and then click the generated link.

Is it safe to scan Telegram QR codes?

Yes, scanning Telegram QR codes is generally safe. The QR codes only contain Telegram link information, and after scanning, you still need to confirm whether to add the user or join the group/channel. However, for safety reasons, we recommend only scanning QR codes from trusted sources.
