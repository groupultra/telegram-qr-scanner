Multiple Ways to Add Friends on Telegram

Telegram offers several methods to add friends, allowing you to choose the most convenient option for different scenarios. This guide will explain in detail how to add friends via username, phone number, QR code, and nearby users.

Whether you're new to Telegram or looking to expand your Telegram social circle more efficiently, this tutorial will help you connect with friends, family, and colleagues on the platform.

Add Friends on Telegram via Username

This is the most common way to add friends, provided you know the person's Telegram username.

1

Open Telegram App

Open the Telegram app on your phone and make sure you're logged in.

2

Tap the Search Icon

On the Telegram main screen, tap the search icon (magnifying glass) in the top right corner.

3

Enter Username

In the search bar, enter the person's Telegram username (starting with @, for example @username).

Note: Telegram usernames are case-sensitive, so make sure you enter it correctly.

4

Tap on Search Result

Find and tap on the user you want to add in the search results.

5

Add Contact

On the user's profile page, tap the Add Contact button to add them as a friend.

Add Friends on Telegram via Phone Number

If you know the person's phone number, you can add them using this method.

1

Open Telegram App

Open the Telegram app on your phone and make sure you're logged in.

2

Tap the New Message Icon

On the Telegram main screen, tap the new message icon (pencil or paper airplane) in the bottom right corner.

3

Tap Add Contact

In the menu that appears, tap the Add Contact option.

4

Enter Phone Number

On the add contact page, enter the person's phone number (including the country/region code).

Note: You can only add someone via phone number if they have allowed themselves to be found by phone number in Telegram.

5

Tap Done

After entering the phone number, tap the Done button. If the number is registered on Telegram, you can add them as a contact.

Add Friends on Telegram via QR Code

This is the fastest way to add friends face-to-face, without typing usernames or phone numbers.

1

Ask the Person to Generate Their QR Code

Ask the person to open Telegram, go to Settings → Profile → tap the three-dot menu in the top right → select QR Code to generate their personal QR code.

2

Open Telegram's QR Scanner

In your Telegram, tap the search icon in the top right, then tap the QR code icon on the right side of the search bar to open the QR scanner.

3

Scan Their QR Code

Point your camera at their QR code, keep it steady, and wait for automatic recognition.

4

Add Contact

After successful scanning, Telegram will display the user's information. Tap the Add Contact button to add them as a friend.

Add Friends on Telegram via Nearby Users

This is a convenient way to add multiple friends at the same location, perfect for meetings, gatherings, and events.

1

Open Telegram App

Open the Telegram app on your phone and make sure you're logged in.

2

Tap the New Message Icon

On the Telegram main screen, tap the new message icon (pencil or paper airplane) in the bottom right corner.

3

Tap People Nearby

In the menu that appears, tap the People Nearby option.

4

Allow Location Permissions

If this is your first time using this feature, Telegram will request location permissions. Tap Allow.

5

View and Add Nearby Users

Telegram will display a list of nearby users who have also enabled this feature. Tap on the user you want to add, then tap the Add Contact button to add them as a friend.

Note: For privacy reasons, it's recommended to tap Stop Sharing when you're done using this feature to turn off location sharing.

Add Friends on Telegram via Username Link

This is a convenient way to add friends remotely, by sharing your link through other social media or messaging platforms.

1

Get Your Telegram Link

Your Telegram link format is: https://t.me/your_username

For example, if your username is @example, your link would be: https://t.me/example

2

Share Your Link

Share this link via text message, email, social media, or other messaging platforms with the friends you want to add.

3

Friend Clicks the Link

When your friend clicks the link you shared, Telegram will automatically open and display your profile.

4

Friend Adds You

Your friend can tap the Message or Add Contact button to add you as a friend.

Frequently Asked Questions About Adding Friends on Telegram

Why can't I find someone by phone number?

Possible reasons include:

  • The phone number is not registered on Telegram
  • The person has disabled the "Find me by phone number" option in their privacy settings
  • You entered the phone number in an incorrect format (make sure to include the country/region code)

How do I manage contacts in Telegram?

In Telegram, you can manage your contacts by following these steps:

  1. Tap the three-line menu in the top left corner or swipe right to open the sidebar
  2. Tap Contacts
  3. In the contacts list, long-press on a contact to edit, delete, or perform other actions

Does the person get notified when I add them as a contact?

Yes, when you add someone as a contact, they will receive a notification. However, this doesn't automatically make you their contact unless they add you back.

How do I block someone on Telegram?

If you no longer want to receive messages from someone, you can block them by following these steps:

  1. Open the chat with the user
  2. Tap their name at the top to open their profile
  3. Tap the three-dot menu in the top right
  4. Select Block User

Set a Personalized Username

In Settings → Privacy and Security, you can control who can find you by phone number, who can see your online status, and more.

Sync Your Contacts

Allow Telegram to access your contacts to automatically find contacts who are already using Telegram. Go to Settings → Contacts → Sync Contacts.
