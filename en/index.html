Frequently Asked Questions

Where is the QR scanner in Telegram?

In the Telegram app, tap the search icon in the top right corner, then you'll see a QR code icon on the right side of the search bar. Tap it to open the QR scanner. In some versions, you can also find the QR code icon in the top right corner of the Contacts page.

Why can't I scan Telegram QR codes?

Possible reasons include: camera permissions not enabled, unclear QR code image, network connection issues, or outdated Telegram app version. We recommend checking these issues, or try using our website to scan and then click the generated link.

How to scan Telegram QR codes on a computer?

You can use our website to scan QR codes on your computer via webcam, upload QR code images, or even directly paste images from your clipboard. After successful scanning, click the generated link to open Telegram on your computer.

How to scan QR codes in Nicegram?

Nicegram is a third-party client for Telegram, and its QR scanning function is similar to the official Telegram app. Click the search icon, then click the QR code icon on the right side of the search bar to open the scanning function. If you encounter issues, you can use our website to scan, then click the link to open in Nicegram.
